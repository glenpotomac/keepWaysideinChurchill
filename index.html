Keep Wayside Elementary in the Churchill Cluster

What’s Happening?

Crown High School is expected to open for the 2027–2028 school year. On December 5, 2024, the Board of Education contracted FLO Analytics to conduct a boundary study to fill the new high school. This study includes several middle and high schools not in close proximity to the new high school. Four proposed options would significantly affect both the Wootton and Churchill clusters. In every proposed option, students from Wayside Elementary would be separated from their Hoover Middle School classmates and reassigned to Wootton High School. These changes could disrupt community stability, hinder student development, and negatively impact mental health.

Why Oppose the Change?

We believe boundary decisions should prioritize students—not just utilization statistics. Reassigning students can have serious unintended consequences.

  • The transition from middle to high school is a critical period for eighth graders:
    • Rapid brain and physical development
    • Increased academic demands
  • Peer continuity strengthens social-emotional resilience
  • Lower-performing students are more vulnerable to disrupted transitions
